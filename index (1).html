01 — About

The man behind

I'm Ryan Bolks — founder of VIAQUANT and a quantitative analyst and trader with 7+ years of experience building systematic strategies across digital assets, equities, and commodities.

My work is rooted in predictive modeling and pattern recognition — applying mathematical frameworks to identify high-probability market behavior before it unfolds. Every analysis I publish is model-driven, not intuition-driven. That's what makes the track record possible.

I hold a B.S. in Financial Management & Predictive Analytics from Oral Roberts University, and serve as an advisory partner at Resound Structured Capital alongside running Viaquant Partners.
